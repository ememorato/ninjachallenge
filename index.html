I've been told life works in a certain way and I should obey the rules. I strongly disagree.

How it all began...

While being in transit through foggy and uncertain times, life led me to a place where things are done differently. A place where freedom combined with intelligence is a royal combination. A place where the future morphs into present, leaving the rest of the world just watching, in awe. It is a place where drive and passion are highly contagious, ready to be absorbed through simple, automatic breathing. This is where my path began. I still don't know what will happen. The fog has not gone away entirely. I'm still moving forward.
